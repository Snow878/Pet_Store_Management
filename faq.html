FAQ | Frequently Asked Questions



What kind of products do you sell?

We do grooming care,vet and also sell grooming care items,apparel and other things needed for care of pest.



Do you allow pets in your stores?


Absolutely! We Love meeting all of your pets and also we take pets if you want to sell them to us.
You can fill in the "sell form" to sell pets to our store. We will surely take proper care.



Can I return an unused and unopened item?


Yes! Our standard return policy is up to 30 days for unopened, unused product. If 30 days have gone
by since your purchase and you feel there was any defect, issue with quality or other concern,
please contact us directly at help@myPet.com and we will do everything in our power to ensure you are 100% satisfied.
To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it
(in its original packaging).



How do I make a return?


To make a return, simply bring the item(s) back to any of your local my Pet's Stuff locations.
If you live an area without a local my-Pet's Stuff location, please send us an email to returns@myPet.com
and we'll work with you to make it right!

Perishable goods such as raw or frozen foods cannot be returned.



How do I get my refund for a returned item in-store?


All refunds for items returned in-store are processed automatically by a credit to your
original form of payment (credit card or cash). Credits to credit cards may take 3-10 days
depending on your financial institution.



Where can I find a my-Pet's Stuff store near me?


Currently we have locations in Marathahalli, Delhi, Indiranagar, Hal, Majestic, Sivaginagar
and Hebbal.
